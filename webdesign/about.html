FAQ

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

What is Dog Shop?

Dog Shop is your premiere online location for finding the perfect dog for you! We have been in business for several decades and we can't wait to unite you with your new best friend!

Who is Dog Shop?

My name is Caleb Forstrom. Me and my wife, Katelyn, run Dog Shop with the help of our thirteen children: Billy, Bobby, Barry, Bella, Bertram, Beau, Bentley, Brandon, Braxton, Brody, Brooks, Bryson, and Bartholomew!

Where is Dog Shop based out of?

We currently breed and raise all of our dogs out of Lithuania because the government is very lenient out there, but we are hoping to relocate to South Dakota.

What is the right dog for me?

Any of them! We believe that you can make any dog work for yourself if you try hard enough and don't get too easily attached!

Does Dog Shop offer refunds?

Sadly, we do not. Buyers assume full responsibility for their dog the moment they send over the payment.

Are Dog Shop dogs properly vaccinated?

We sure hope so! Again, where we are, the government is very lenient in dog regulations, so we are not too sure!
